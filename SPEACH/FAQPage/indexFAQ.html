FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

General Questions

What is Speach ?

Speach is an online learning website that is specialized on helping people all around the world study languages on a much more detail leveled. Speach is generally made by a few college students in Indonesia for a Human Computer and Interaction semester project. Our goal is to provide a free and engaging website for Speach users, especially to fulfills the customer’s needs, and give effective functionality whenever they use the site. In addition to that, We would also try to give a new approach on the design with a more ‘peach’ and ‘aesthetic’ theme rather than sticking the stereotype ‘modern’ and ‘simple’ style

Can I actually be fluent in different languages using Speach

We believe that anyone can do whatever they want to do if they put the effort to it. The same principle goes with this website, we can’t give you promises that if you use this website you will certainly can speak the languages that you desire, but we build this website for the purpose if giving the people a substitution and a new way of learning languages without having the constraints of money, transportation, geographical dependency, etc. If you’re willing to trust and use our website according to our guide, then you definitely can be fluent in your designated languages, the result will show itself! So cheer up, and hwaiting !
